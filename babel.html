About This Project

Babel is a visual novel game made within 48 hours based on the theme "Rebirth." You awaken and explore an abandoned luxury space cruise as Babel, an AI customer agent. As you explore, you regain your lost memories and try to make sense of what happened.

Programmer & Artist

I designed two of the characters in the game and drew their game sprites. I explored coding various features in Ren'Py, such as a lock restriction on a room unless all other rooms have been visited, and character motions, such as jump/bounce to provide a more immersive playing experience.
