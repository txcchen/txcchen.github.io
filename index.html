Councillor Worrell x Small Businesses Spark! Project

Oct - Dec 2022
Github

Data Analyst

Collaborated with a team of 4 to analyze BPDA, City of Boston MBE and relevant datasets using Pandas and GeoPandas to understand the current landscape of small businesses in Boston District 4, determine current issues small businesses face and ways to attract small businesses to District 4. We identified barriers and successes of District 4 in comparison to other districts, as well as reasonable benchmarks for small businesses and submitted a written report to City Councilor Worrell.
